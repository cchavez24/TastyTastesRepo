About Ash

Ash is an amazing chef and baker. She is heavily influenced by the flavors and cuisine of Italy. Having spent several summers in her homeland changed the way she looked at food. She spent that time soaking in the flavors and is now bringing the public all the knowledge she acquired. In her downtime she enjoys going on hikes and finding cheap airfares.
